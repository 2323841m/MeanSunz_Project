About

Welcome to meansunz! This is a gamified bulletin board, where the aim is to become the most popular on the site. Your popularity score depends on how many "upvotes" you get on all of your posts. You can check the leaderboards to see who the most popular people are! To start posting you must first of all register and login. Then you can access any category that interests you and start posting!
